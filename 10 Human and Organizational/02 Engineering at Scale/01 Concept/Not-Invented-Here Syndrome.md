# Not-Invented-Here Syndrome

> <- Back to [[Organizational Antipatterns]]

Rejecting external solutions in favor of building custom replacements, even when existing solutions are adequate. Leads to maintenance burden, wheel reinvention, and slower delivery. Often driven by pride or distrust of external code.

# Staff Engineer Archetypes

> <- Back to [[Technical Leadership]]

Will Larson's four archetypes: Tech Lead (guides a team), Architect (responsible for technical direction of a domain), Solver (parachutes into hard problems), Right Hand (extends an executive's reach). Most staff engineers blend archetypes.

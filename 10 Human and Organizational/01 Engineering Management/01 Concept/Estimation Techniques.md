# Estimation Techniques

> <- Back to [[Planning and Execution]]

Methods for estimating effort: story points (relative sizing), t-shirt sizing (S/M/L/XL), #NoEstimates (just break work small enough), reference-based (compare to past work). All estimates are wrong; the goal is useful approximations.

# Onboarding Engineers

> <- Back to [[Hiring and Growing Engineers]]

Structured programs to help new engineers become productive: 30/60/90 day plans with clear milestones, buddy system for questions, ramp-up projects of increasing scope. Good onboarding dramatically affects retention and time-to-productivity.

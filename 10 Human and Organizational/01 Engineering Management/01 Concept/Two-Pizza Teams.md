# Two-Pizza Teams

> <- Back to [[Team Structure]]

Amazon's principle that teams should be small enough to be fed by two pizzas (6-10 people). Small, autonomous teams minimize communication overhead, move faster, and maintain clear ownership. Larger teams suffer from coordination costs that grow quadratically.

# SBI Feedback Model

> <- Back to [[Conflict Resolution and Feedback]]

Structured feedback delivery: Situation (when/where it happened), Behavior (what specifically was observed), Impact (the effect of the behavior). Separates observation from judgment and makes feedback specific and actionable.

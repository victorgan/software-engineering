# OKRs

> <- Back to [[Planning and Execution]]

Objectives and Key Results. A goal-setting framework: Objectives are qualitative goals (what you want to achieve); Key Results are measurable outcomes (how you know you achieved it). Should be ambitious, aligned across the organization, and reviewed regularly.

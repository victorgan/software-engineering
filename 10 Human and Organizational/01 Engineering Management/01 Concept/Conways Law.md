# Conway's Law

> <- Back to [[Team Structure]]

"Organizations design systems that mirror their communication structure." This observation has profound implications: if you want a microservices architecture, you need small, autonomous teams. The Inverse Conway Maneuver deliberately designs team structure to produce the desired architecture.

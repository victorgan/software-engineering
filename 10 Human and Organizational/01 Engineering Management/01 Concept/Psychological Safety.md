# Psychological Safety

> <- Back to [[Engineering Culture]]

A team environment where members feel safe to take risks, admit mistakes, ask questions, and challenge ideas without fear of punishment or humiliation. Google's Project Aristotle found it to be the #1 predictor of team effectiveness.

# Tech Lead vs Engineering Manager

> <- Back to [[Team Structure]]

Two complementary leadership roles. Tech Lead: sets technical direction, makes architectural decisions, ensures code quality. Engineering Manager: people management, career development, team health, shielding from distractions. Some organizations combine these; most separate them at scale.

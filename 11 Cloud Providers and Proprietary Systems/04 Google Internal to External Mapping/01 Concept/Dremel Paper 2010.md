# Dremel Paper (2010)

> <- Back to [[Key Papers to Read]]

**"Dremel: Interactive Analysis of Web-Scale Datasets" (2010)** -- Melnik et al.

Introduced columnar storage for nested data with a tree-shaped distributed execution engine. Enabled interactive (seconds-level) SQL queries on petabyte-scale datasets. Became the foundation for BigQuery (GCP). Inspired Apache Drill, Presto/Trino, and Apache Impala.

# Pub Sub

> <- Back to [[Message Queues and Event Systems]]

Publish-Subscribe pattern where messages are published to a topic and delivered to all subscribers. Each subscriber gets its own copy. Enables decoupled architectures where publishers do not know about subscribers. Examples include Kafka topics, SNS, and Google Pub/Sub.

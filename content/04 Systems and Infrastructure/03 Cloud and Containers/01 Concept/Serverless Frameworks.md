# Serverless Frameworks

> <- Back to [[Serverless]]

Tools that simplify developing, testing, and deploying serverless applications. Serverless Framework (multi-cloud, YAML-based), AWS SAM (AWS-native, CloudFormation extension), and SST (TypeScript-first, live development). These frameworks abstract away boilerplate and provide local development capabilities.

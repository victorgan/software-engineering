# Physical Layer

> <- Back to [[OSI and TCP-IP Model]]

The lowest layer dealing with the physical transmission of raw bits over a communication medium. Includes cables (copper, fiber optic), wireless signals (Wi-Fi, cellular), connectors, and electrical/optical signaling specifications.

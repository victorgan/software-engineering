# Retraining Triggers

> <- Back to [[Model Monitoring]]

Criteria that determine when a model should be retrained. Scheduled (periodic retraining), performance-based (accuracy drops below threshold), drift-based (data or concept drift detected). Automated retraining pipelines reduce time to recovery.

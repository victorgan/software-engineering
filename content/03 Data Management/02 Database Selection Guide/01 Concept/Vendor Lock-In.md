# Vendor Lock-In

> <- Back to [[Key Decision Factors]]

Postgres, MySQL, and Redis are portable across cloud providers. DynamoDB, Firestore, and Cosmos DB are cloud-specific. Consider multi-cloud requirements when selecting a database.

# Discrete Mathematics

**Back:** [[Mathematics for CS]]

The study of mathematical structures that are fundamentally discrete rather than continuous. Discrete mathematics is the mathematical backbone of computer science, providing the language and tools for reasoning about computation, algorithms, and data structures.

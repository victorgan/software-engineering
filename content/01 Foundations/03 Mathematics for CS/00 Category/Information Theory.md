# Information Theory

**Back:** [[Mathematics for CS]]

The mathematical study of quantifying, storing, and communicating information. Founded by Claude Shannon, information theory provides fundamental limits on data compression and reliable communication, and has applications across coding theory, cryptography, and machine learning.

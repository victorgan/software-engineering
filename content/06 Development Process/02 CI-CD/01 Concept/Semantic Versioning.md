# Semantic Versioning

> <- Back to [[Release Engineering]]

MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH versioning scheme. MAJOR for breaking changes, MINOR for backward-compatible features, PATCH for backward-compatible fixes. Communicates the nature of changes to consumers and enables dependency resolution.

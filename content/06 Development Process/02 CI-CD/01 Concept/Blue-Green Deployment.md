# Blue-Green Deployment

> <- Back to [[Deployment Strategies]]

Two identical environments: Blue (current) and Green (new). Deploy to Green, verify, then switch traffic from Blue to Green instantly. Provides instant rollback by switching back to Blue. Requires double the infrastructure.

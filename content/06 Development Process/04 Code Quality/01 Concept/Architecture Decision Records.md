# Architecture Decision Records

> <- Back to [[Technical Debt]]

Document decisions and their rationale. ADRs capture the context, decision, consequences, and alternatives considered for significant architectural choices. They prevent re-debating settled decisions and help newcomers understand why the system is built the way it is.

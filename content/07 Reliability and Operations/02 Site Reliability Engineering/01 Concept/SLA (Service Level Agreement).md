# SLA (Service Level Agreement)

> <- Back to [[SLAs, SLOs, SLIs]]

A contractual commitment between a service provider and its customers with defined consequences for violations (typically financial penalties or service credits). SLAs should be less aggressive than internal SLOs to provide a safety buffer.

# Health Checks

> <- Back to [[Reliability Patterns]]

Endpoints that report whether a service is operational and ready to serve traffic. Kubernetes defines three types: liveness probes (is the process alive?), readiness probes (can it handle requests?), and startup probes (has it finished initializing?).

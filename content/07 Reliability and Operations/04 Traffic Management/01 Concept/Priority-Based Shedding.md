# Priority-Based Shedding

> <- Back to [[Load Shedding]]

Dropping low-priority requests under load to protect critical paths. Requests are classified by importance (e.g., checkout > browsing, health checks > background jobs), and lower-priority traffic is shed first when the system approaches capacity.

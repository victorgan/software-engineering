# Traffic Mirroring

> <- Back to [[Traffic Shifting]]

Duplicating production traffic and sending it to a test environment without affecting real users. Also known as traffic shadowing, it enables testing new versions with real traffic patterns and data, catching issues that synthetic tests miss.

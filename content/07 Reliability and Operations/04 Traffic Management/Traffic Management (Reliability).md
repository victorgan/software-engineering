# Traffic Management (Reliability)

**Back:** [[07 - Reliability and Operations MOC]]

Traffic management controls how requests flow through a system, including shifting traffic between deployments, shedding load under pressure, and managing runtime configuration. Effective traffic management is essential for safe deployments, graceful degradation, and operational control of production systems.

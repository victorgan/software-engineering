# Alert Fatigue

> <- Back to [[Dashboards and Alerting]]

When too many alerts fire, operators become desensitized and start ignoring them, leading to missed real incidents. Alert fatigue is one of the most common operational anti-patterns and must be actively managed through alert hygiene, tuning, and periodic review.

# Percentiles

> <- Back to [[Metrics]]

Statistical measures that describe the distribution of values. p50 (median), p95, p99, and p99.9 are far more informative than averages for understanding latency, as averages hide tail latency that affects real users. High percentiles reveal the worst-case experience.

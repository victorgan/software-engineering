# OpenTelemetry

> <- Back to [[Distributed Tracing]]

Vendor-neutral open standard for collecting traces, metrics, and logs. OpenTelemetry (OTel) provides APIs, SDKs, and the OpenTelemetry Collector for instrumenting, generating, collecting, and exporting telemetry data, enabling portability across observability backends.

# Chaos Monkey

> <- Back to [[Chaos Engineering]]

Netflix's tool that randomly terminates production instances to ensure services are resilient to individual instance failures. Chaos Monkey pioneered the practice of proactive failure injection and demonstrated that regular failures build more resilient systems.

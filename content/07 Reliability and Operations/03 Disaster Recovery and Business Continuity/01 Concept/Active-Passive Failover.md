# Active-Passive Failover

> <- Back to [[Failover Strategies]]

A failover strategy where a standby system takes over when the primary fails. Variants include cold standby (turned off, slow to start), warm standby (running but not serving traffic), and hot standby (synchronized and ready for immediate failover).

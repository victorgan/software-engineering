# Point-in-Time Recovery

> <- Back to [[Backup and Restore]]

The ability to restore a database to any specific moment in time by replaying Write-Ahead Log (WAL) entries on top of a base backup. PITR enables near-zero RPO and is essential for recovering from logical errors like accidental data deletion.

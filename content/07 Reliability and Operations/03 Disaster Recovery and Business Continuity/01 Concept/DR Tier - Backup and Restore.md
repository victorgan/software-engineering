# DR Tier - Backup and Restore

> <- Back to [[Disaster Recovery Tiers]]

The simplest and cheapest DR tier. Regular backups are stored offsite and restored when needed. Recovery takes hours as infrastructure must be provisioned and data restored. Suitable for non-critical systems with tolerant RTO/RPO requirements.

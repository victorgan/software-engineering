# Active-Active Failover

> <- Back to [[Failover Strategies]]

A failover strategy where multiple regions actively serve traffic simultaneously. Provides near-zero downtime since traffic can be shifted away from a failed region. Requires conflict resolution for concurrent writes and adds significant complexity.

# DR Drills

> <- Back to [[Business Continuity]]

Regular simulated failovers and tabletop exercises that test disaster recovery procedures. Drills validate that runbooks work, that teams know their roles, and that recovery meets RTO and RPO targets. Unexercised plans are unreliable plans.

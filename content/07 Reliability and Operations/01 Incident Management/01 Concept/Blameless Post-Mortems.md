# Blameless Post-Mortems

> <- Back to [[Post-Incident Process]]

Post-incident reviews that focus on systems and processes rather than blaming individuals. Blameless post-mortems create psychological safety that encourages honest reporting, leading to better understanding of systemic issues and more effective remediation.

- [[Psychological Safety]]
- [[Systems Thinking]]

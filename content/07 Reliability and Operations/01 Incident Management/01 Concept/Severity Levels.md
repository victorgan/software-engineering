# Severity Levels

> <- Back to [[Incident Response]]

Classification of incidents by impact and urgency. SEV1 (critical, widespread user impact) through SEV4 (minor, minimal impact). Severity determines response urgency, communication requirements, escalation paths, and post-incident review depth.

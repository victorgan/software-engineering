# Learning Culture

> <- Back to [[Post-Incident Process]]

An organizational culture that shares post-mortems widely and celebrates learning from failure. Publishing post-mortems internally, running incident review meetings, and recognizing teams that improve reliability transforms incidents from shame into organizational knowledge.

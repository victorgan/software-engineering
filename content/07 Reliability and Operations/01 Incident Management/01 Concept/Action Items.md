# Action Items

> <- Back to [[Post-Incident Process]]

Concrete, assigned, tracked remediation tasks that emerge from post-mortem analysis. Effective action items have clear owners, deadlines, and are prioritized by impact. Without follow-through on action items, post-mortems are theater.

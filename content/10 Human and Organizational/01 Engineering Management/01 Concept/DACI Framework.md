# DACI Framework

> <- Back to [[Decision-Making]]

A decision-making framework that clarifies roles: Driver (owns the process), Approver (makes the final call), Contributors (provide input), Informed (need to know the outcome). Prevents decisions from stalling due to unclear ownership.

# Team Topologies

> <- Back to [[Team Structure]]

A framework for organizing teams: stream-aligned (deliver value for a specific flow), enabling (help other teams adopt capabilities), complicated subsystem (deep specialist expertise), platform (provide self-service capabilities). From the book by Matthew Skelton and Manuel Pais.

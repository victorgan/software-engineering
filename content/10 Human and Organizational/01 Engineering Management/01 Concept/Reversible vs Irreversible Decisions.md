# Reversible vs Irreversible Decisions

> <- Back to [[Decision-Making]]

Jeff Bezos's framework: "one-way doors" (irreversible decisions like public API contracts, database schemas) deserve careful deliberation. "Two-way doors" (reversible decisions like feature flags, internal tool choices) should be made quickly. Most decisions are two-way doors.

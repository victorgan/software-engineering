# Leadership Skill Progression

> <- Back to [[Core Skills by Level]]

Self-management (junior) -> team influence (mid) -> org influence (senior) -> company/industry influence (staff+). Leadership is not about titles; it is about expanding your sphere of impact and multiplying the output of those around you.

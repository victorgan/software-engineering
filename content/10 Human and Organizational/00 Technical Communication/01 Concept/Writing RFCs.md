# Writing RFCs

> <- Back to [[Writing for Engineers]]

Structure: problem statement (what problem are we solving and why now), proposed solution (technical details), alternatives considered (what else was evaluated), risks (what could go wrong), and open questions. Good RFCs drive alignment and surface concerns early.

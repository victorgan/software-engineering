# Commit Messages

> <- Back to [[Writing for Engineers]]

Write in imperative mood ("Add feature" not "Added feature"). Follow conventional commits format (feat:, fix:, chore:). Explain why, not just what. A good commit history tells the story of how the codebase evolved.

# Dunbar's Numbers in Engineering

> <- Back to [[Scaling Engineering Organizations]]

Communication overhead grows O(n^2) with team size. Dunbar's number (~150) is the cognitive limit for maintaining social relationships. Small teams (5-8) stay effective; larger groups need formal structure, clear interfaces, and reduced cross-team dependencies.

# Build vs Buy

> <- Back to [[Scaling Engineering Organizations]]

Framework for deciding when to build custom vs purchase/use existing solutions. Consider: is it core competency? Time to market pressure? Long-term maintenance burden? Vendor risk and lock-in? Most companies build too much.

# Resume-Driven Development

> <- Back to [[Organizational Antipatterns]]

Choosing technology for career advancement rather than problem fit. "Let's use Kubernetes for our 100-user app" or "Let's rewrite in Rust." The technology should serve the problem, not the engineer's resume.

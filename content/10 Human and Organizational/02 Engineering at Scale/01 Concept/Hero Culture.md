# Hero Culture

> <- Back to [[Organizational Antipatterns]]

Dependency on individual heroes who save the day during crises. Fragile, leads to burnout, and creates single points of failure. Healthy organizations distribute knowledge, automate responses, and design systems that do not require heroics.

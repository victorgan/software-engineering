# Strangler Fig

> <- Back to [[Monolith]]

Incrementally migrate a monolith to microservices. Named after the strangler fig tree that grows around and eventually replaces its host. Route traffic to new services as they're built, gradually replacing monolith functionality until the original system can be decommissioned.

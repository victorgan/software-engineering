# Megastore Paper (2011)

> <- Back to [[Key Papers to Read]]

**"Megastore: Providing Scalable, Highly Available Storage for Interactive Services" (2011)** -- Baker et al.

Bridged NoSQL scalability with relational features. Introduced entity groups for transactional consistency within partitions and Paxos-based replication across data centers. Predecessor to Spanner. Historical importance in the evolution from Bigtable to Spanner.

**"Zanzibar: Google's Consistent, Global Authorization System" (2019)** -- Pang et al.

Describes Google's global authorization system that checks billions of access control decisions per second. Key contributions: relationship-based access control (ReBAC) model, namespace configuration language, new enemy problem handling, and global consistency with low latency. Inspired SpiceDB, OpenFGA, and Ory Keto.

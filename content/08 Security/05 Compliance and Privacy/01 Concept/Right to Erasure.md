# Right to Erasure

> <- Back to [[Privacy Engineering]]

A user's right to request deletion of their personal data (GDPR Article 17). Implementation challenges include soft delete vs hard delete, propagation to backups, third-party data processors, and data that is technically difficult to locate across distributed systems.

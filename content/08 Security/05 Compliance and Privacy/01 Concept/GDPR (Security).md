# GDPR (Security)

> <- Back to [[Regulatory Frameworks]]

The EU General Data Protection Regulation. Grants data subject rights (access, erasure, portability), requires a Data Protection Officer (DPO), mandates consent for data processing, and requires breach notification within 72 hours. Applies to any organization processing EU residents' data.

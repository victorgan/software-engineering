# Privacy Engineering

**Back:** [[Compliance and Privacy]]

Engineering practices that protect user privacy by design rather than as an afterthought. Privacy engineering encompasses data minimization, anonymization, consent management, and the right to erasure, building privacy into the architecture from the start.

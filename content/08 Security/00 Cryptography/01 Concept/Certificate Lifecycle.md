# Certificate Lifecycle

> <- Back to [[PKI (Public Key Infrastructure)]]

The stages of a certificate's life: issuance (CSR, validation, signing), deployment, monitoring for expiration, renewal, and revocation (via CRL or OCSP). Automated certificate management prevents outages caused by expired certificates.

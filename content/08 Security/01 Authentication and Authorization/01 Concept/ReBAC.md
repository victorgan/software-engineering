# ReBAC

> <- Back to [[Authorization Models]]

Relationship-Based Access Control makes authorization decisions based on relationships between entities. Inspired by Google's Zanzibar system, ReBAC excels at modeling complex permissions like "user can edit this document because they are a member of the team that owns the folder containing it."

# DDoS Protection

> <- Back to [[Network Security]]

Defending against Distributed Denial of Service attacks that overwhelm systems with traffic. Protection layers include rate limiting at the application level, CDN-based absorption (CloudFlare), and cloud provider shields (AWS Shield). Traffic scrubbing separates legitimate traffic from attack traffic.

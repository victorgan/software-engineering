# Scale

> <- Back to [[Key Decision Factors]]

Vertical scaling sufficient (<10TB, <100K QPS) favors Postgres/MySQL. Horizontal scaling needed for larger workloads favors DynamoDB, Cassandra, CockroachDB, or Vitess (MySQL sharding).

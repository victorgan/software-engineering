# Mathematics for CS

**Back:** [[01 - Foundations MOC]]

Mathematics provides the formal foundation for computer science. Discrete mathematics, linear algebra, probability, and information theory underpin algorithms, data structures, machine learning, cryptography, and nearly every other area of computing. A solid mathematical foundation enables rigorous reasoning about correctness and performance.

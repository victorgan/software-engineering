# Privacy by Design

> <- Back to [[Privacy Engineering]]

Building privacy protections into system architecture from the start, not as an afterthought. Privacy by design principles include proactive not reactive, privacy as default, privacy embedded in design, full functionality (not zero-sum), end-to-end security, and user-centric design.

# Differential Privacy

> <- Back to [[Privacy Engineering]]

A mathematical framework that provides provable privacy guarantees. Differential privacy ensures that the output of a query does not significantly change whether any individual's data is included or excluded. Used by Apple, Google, and the US Census for privacy-preserving analytics.

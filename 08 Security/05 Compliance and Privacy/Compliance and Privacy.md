# Compliance & Privacy

**Back:** [[08 - Security MOC]]

Compliance and privacy encompass the regulatory frameworks, engineering practices, and organizational processes required to protect user data and meet legal obligations. From GDPR and SOC 2 to privacy by design, these disciplines ensure that systems handle data responsibly and lawfully.

# RBAC

> <- Back to [[Authorization Models]]

Role-Based Access Control assigns users to roles, and roles to permissions. Users inherit all permissions of their assigned roles. RBAC is simple to understand and manage, making it the most commonly used authorization model. Example: Admin, Editor, Viewer roles.

# AES

> <- Back to [[Symmetric Encryption]]

Advanced Encryption Standard, the most widely used symmetric encryption algorithm. Available in AES-128, AES-192, and AES-256 key sizes. AES is a block cipher that operates on 128-bit blocks. AES-256 is recommended for most applications.

# Toil

> <- Back to [[On-Call]]

Repetitive, manual, automatable operational work that scales linearly with service size and provides no enduring value. Toil is the enemy of engineering productivity. Identifying and eliminating toil frees teams to work on projects that improve reliability and reduce future toil.

# Weighted Routing

> <- Back to [[Traffic Shifting]]

Gradually shifting traffic between deployments by adjusting weights (e.g., send 5% to the new version, then 25%, then 50%, then 100%). Used for canary deployments and blue-green transitions. See also [[CI-CD]].

# Burn Rate Alerts

> <- Back to [[Error Budgets]]

Alerts that fire when the error budget is being consumed faster than sustainable. Multi-window, multi-burn-rate alerting (as described in Google's SRE Workbook) provides early warning of reliability problems by detecting both fast burns (incidents) and slow burns (gradual degradation).

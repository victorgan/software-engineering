# Chaos Engineering

**Back:** [[Performance Engineering]]

The discipline of proactively introducing failures into production systems to build confidence in their resilience. Chaos engineering follows a scientific approach: define steady state, hypothesize, inject failure, and observe whether the system maintains its SLOs.

# Dashboards and Alerting

**Back:** [[Observability]]

Dashboards visualize system health and performance, while alerting notifies operators of conditions requiring attention. Effective dashboards prioritize actionable information, and effective alerting focuses on symptoms over causes to minimize alert fatigue.

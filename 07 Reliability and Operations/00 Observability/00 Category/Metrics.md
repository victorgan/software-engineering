# Metrics

**Back:** [[Observability]]

Metrics are numeric measurements collected over time that provide quantitative insight into system behavior. From counters and gauges to histograms, metrics enable real-time monitoring, alerting, and capacity planning through established methodologies like RED, USE, and the Four Golden Signals.

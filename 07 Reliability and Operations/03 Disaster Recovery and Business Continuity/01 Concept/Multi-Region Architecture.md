# Multi-Region Architecture

> <- Back to [[Failover Strategies]]

Deploying services and data across multiple geographic regions for the highest level of fault tolerance. Includes cross-region replication, regional isolation, and global load balancing. Protects against entire region outages but adds significant latency and consistency challenges.

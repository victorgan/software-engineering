# Failover Strategies

**Back:** [[Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity]]

Mechanisms for switching from a failed primary system to a standby or backup system. Strategies range from active-passive (cold/warm/hot standby) to active-active (multiple active regions), each with different cost, complexity, and recovery time characteristics.

# Backup and Restore

**Back:** [[Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity]]

The foundational disaster recovery strategy of creating copies of data and being able to restore them. Defined by RPO (how much data can you lose) and RTO (how long can you be down), backup strategies range from simple full backups to continuous point-in-time recovery.

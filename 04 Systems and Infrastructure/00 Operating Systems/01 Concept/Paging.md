# Paging

> <- Back to [[Memory Management]]

Divides virtual and physical memory into fixed-size blocks (pages and frames, typically 4 KB). Enables non-contiguous allocation, demand loading, and efficient swapping. Page faults trigger loading pages from disk into RAM.

# Wait-Free Algorithms

> <- Back to [[Lock-Free & Wait-Free Programming]]

Algorithms where every thread completes its operation in a bounded number of steps, regardless of the actions of other threads. Wait-free is a stronger guarantee than lock-free: not just system-wide progress, but individual thread progress is guaranteed.

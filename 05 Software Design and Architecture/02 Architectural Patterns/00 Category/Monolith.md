# Monolith

**Back:** [[Architectural Patterns]]

A single deployable unit containing the entire application. Monoliths are the simplest architectural style and the right choice for many applications, especially early-stage products and small teams. A well-structured modular monolith can provide clean boundaries without distributed system complexity.

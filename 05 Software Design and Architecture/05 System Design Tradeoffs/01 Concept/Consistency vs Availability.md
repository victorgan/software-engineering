# Consistency vs Availability

> <- Back to [[Fundamental Tradeoffs]]

CAP theorem in practice: under network partition, you must choose between consistency (every read gets the most recent write) and availability (every request gets a response). Strong consistency costs availability; eventual consistency improves availability.
